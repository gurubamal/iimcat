Blackstone has elevated Head of Real Estate in India Tuhin Parikh to Vice-Chairman of Real Estate Asia and Executive Chairman of Real Estate India, while Asheesh Mohta will step into Parikh’s role, a statement said.
Mohta will report to Parikh, who will continue to report to Chris Heady, Chairman of APAC & Head of Real Estate Asia.
“This is part of broader changes to our APAC Real Estate business – which under Chris’ leadership over the last 20 years has become one of the largest, most significant platforms of its kind in the region, including In India,” the statement said.
Blackstone manages assets of over $50 billion in India since inception, of which $20 billion is in real estate.
The US-based alternate assets manager is one of the leading foreign real estate investors in the country and is the largest owner of commercial office space and malls portfolio.
