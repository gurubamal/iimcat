Title   : LTTS partners with SiMa.ai to develop physical AI solutions

Mumbai
L&T Technology Services Ltd announced a strategic partnership with Silicon Valley-based SiMa.ai on Tuesday to develop AI-driven solutions across mobility, healthcare, industrial automation and robotics sectors.
The collaboration will integrate LTTS’ engineering expertise with SiMa.ai’s MLSoC™ ONE platform and software development kit to create scalable Physical AI applications. The partnership targets specific areas including in-vehicle infotainment systems, advanced driver assistance systems, industrial automation, robotics and healthcare technologies.
Under the agreement, SiMa.ai will leverage LTTS’ core engineering capabilities and workforce investments to deliver AI hardware and software solutions. The companies plan to jointly develop breakthrough use cases that will expand adoption of SiMa.ai’s platform across multiple industries.
“SiMa.ai cements LTTS’ position as a leader in AI-led ER&D solutions,” said Amit Chadha, CEO and Managing Director of L&T Technology Services. The partnership aims to help clients accelerate time-to-market and enhance operational efficiency.
Krishna Rangasayee, Founder and CEO of SiMa.ai, emphasized the collaboration’s potential to scale Physical AI applications globally, citing LTTS’ engineering capabilities and client reach as key factors.
The partnership includes establishing a scalable product support framework and joint go-to-market strategies to accelerate AI technology adoption worldwide. LTTS, a subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro, serves 69 Fortune 500 companies and operates across 23 global design centers.
The shares of L&T Technology Services Ltd were trading on the NSE today at ₹4,186 down by ₹20.90 or 0.50 per cent at 11.55 am.

Title   : Waaree Energies shares in focus on Rs 192 crore acquisition of 64% stake in Kotson’s Pvt Ltd 
Source  : economictimes.indiatimes.com
Published: 2025-09-03T04:25:04
Fetched : 2025-09-03T10:52:34.736767
URL     : https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/markets/stocks/news/waaree-energies-shares-in-focus-on-rs-192-crore-acquisition-of-64-stake-in-kotsons-pvt-ltd/articleshow/123669214.cms
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As per the regulatory disclosure dated September 2, 2025, the acquisition will be executed through a Share Subscription and Shareholders Agreement, with the deal expected to close by September 30, or such extended date as may be mutually agreed upon by the parties.
Founded on April 22, 1978, Kotson’s Private Ltd is engaged in the business of designing, manufacturing, and supplying advanced transformer solutions. The company has built a strong presence in India’s electrical equipment sector, offering a wide array of transformer types, including Oil Filled, Dry Type, Special Purpose, and Renewable Energy application transformers.
The company’s manufacturing facility currently has a production capacity of 4,000 MVA.
The acquisition of Kotson’s is expected to allow Waaree Energies to strengthen its position in the power value chain by enhancing its control over supply and product quality.
The transaction does not fall under the related party category, and Waaree Energies will acquire 2.56 crore shares at Rs 75 per share (face value Rs 10 each), marking a significant investment in scaling up its backend manufacturing capabilities.
Once the deal is concluded, Kotson’s Private Limited will become a subsidiary of Waaree Energies.
In addition to Kotson’s deal, Waaree Energies also approved the acquisition of 100% shareholding in Impactgrid Renewables. The latter is a newly incorporated, non-operational entity engaged in the renewable energy space and is currently a step-down subsidiary of the company.
This acquisition, executed for a consideration of Rs 1 lakh, according to the exchange filing.
