Hyundai share price live today: announces price reduction in its vehicles to pass on GST cut benefits
Hyundai Motor India Ltd (HMIL) announced on Sunday that it will pass on the full benefit of the recent GST rate cut on passenger vehicles to its customers, effective from September 22, when the revised rates take effect.
This move will make Hyundai cars more affordable for buyers across India, adding to the festive cheer of owning a new vehicle, the company said in a statement.
The price cuts vary across models, ranging from ₹60,640 on the Verna to ₹2,40,303 on the Tucson. Other notable reductions include ₹98,053 on the i20, ₹1,23,659 on the Venue, and ₹72,145 on the Creta, according to the release.
