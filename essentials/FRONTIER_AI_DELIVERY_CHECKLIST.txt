6. ALPHA FORMULA
================================================================================

Formula:
  Alpha = (25×MOM20 + 15×MOM60 + 10×RVOL + 10×SqueezeBO 
           + 10×PBZ + 20×NewsScore + 5×TrendBonus) / 95
  
  Saturated = 50 + 50×tanh((Alpha/50) - 1)
  Final = clip(Saturated, 0, 100)

Weights:
  - 40% to momentum (MOM20 + MOM60)
  - 20% to volume (RVOL)
  - 20% to news (NewsScore)
  - 20% to setup (SqueezeBO + PBZ + TrendBonus)

Gate Threshold: Alpha ≥ 70 (high conviction only)

10. RISK MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

ATR-Based Position Sizing:
  Stop Loss:  Entry - 1.5×ATR20
  TP1:        Entry + 1.5×ATR20 (Sell 50%)
  TP2:        Entry + 3×ATR20 (Sell 25%)
  Trailing:   max(Trail, Close - 2.5×ATR20) (Hold 25%)

2% Risk Rule:
  Max Risk = Account × 2%
  Shares = Max Risk / (Entry - Stop)
  Position = Shares × Entry Price

Hard Rules:
  - Stop loss is absolute (no exceptions)
  - Position sizing strictly 2%
  - No averaging down after stop
  - Max 2 losses = stop trading day
