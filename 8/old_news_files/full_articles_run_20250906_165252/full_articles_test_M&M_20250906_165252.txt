Indian automaker Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) announced on September 6 that it would pass on the reduced GST benefits to consumers.
The vehicle prices will be slashed between ₹1,01,000 and ₹1,45,000 depending on the vehicle models.
“Mahindra & Mahindra will pass on the full GST benefits to customers across its entire Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) SUV portfolio. This follows the announcement of GST 2.0 at the 56th GST Council meeting held on September 3, 2025, and headed by Nirmala Sitharaman, Minister of Finance, Government of India.
The benefits will be available to customers on the ICE portfolios starting from September 6, 2025,” the company mentioned in a statement.
