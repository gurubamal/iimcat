Title   : IREDA posts 42% rise in Q2 profit on clean energy loans growth 
Source  : www.thehindubusinessline.com
Published: 2025-10-14T06:47:38
Fetched : 2025-10-14T14:53:03.035092
URL     : https://www.thehindubusinessline.com/companies/ireda-posts-42-rise-in-q2-profit-on-clean-energy-loans-growth/article70161553.ece
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Indian Renewable Energy Development Agency on Tuesday posted a 42% growth in its second quarter profit, helped by robust green lending growth.
The renewable energy financier's profit grew to ₹549 crore ($61.8 million) in the quarter ended September 30 from ₹388 crore in the year-ago period.
Its interest income rose 28.7% to ₹2,030 crore .
The firm's earnings come as major industry players in India in expand their renewable energy capacity, both with an eye on exports and as India targets 500 gigawatts of non-fossil fuel capacity by 2030.
IREDA's gross loan book stood at ₹76,282 crores ($8.6 billion) as of fiscal 2025, with solar energy projects making up the largest share, followed by wind. This positions the company as a key financier in India’s clean energy transition.
While IREDA is the only NBFC in India solely focused on green energy, it competes with broader power sector lenders like Power Finance Corporation and REC, which are yet to report earnings.
Shares of IREDA rose 2% following the results.
