Kalpataru Ltd reported pre-sales of ₹1,329 crore in Q2 FY26, marking a 19 per cent year-on-year increase from ₹1,117 crore in the corresponding quarter last year, according to operational updates disclosed to stock exchanges by the company.
The Mumbai-based company’s collections showed strong momentum, rising 37 per cent to ₹1,162 crore in Q2 FY26, compared to ₹849 crore in Q2 FY25. This acceleration in collections indicates improved cash flow generation during the quarter.
For the first half of fiscal year 2026, Kalpataru’s performance was notably robust. Pre-sales reached ₹2,577 crore, up 43 per cent from ₹1,799 crore in H1 FY25. Collections for the half-year period stood at ₹2,308 crore, maintaining the 37 per cent growth rate seen in the quarterly figures, compared to ₹1,687 crore in the year-ago period.
The company cautioned that the figures are provisional and subject to limited review.
The shares of Kalpataru Ltd ended at ₹398.85 on the NSE today, up by ₹30.05 or 8.15 per cent.
